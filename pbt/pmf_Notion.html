Notion

Last updated Aug 29th '25
  • Based on Electron; so all its pluses/minuses (no Android support, slow, too many resources used...). Surprisingly NO Linux ver avail. Mobile ver sluggish.
  • It is a hierarchical note-taker (@rsch)
  • V. flexible: designed to combine various progs a biz might use for diff funcs in diff depts.
  • Usrs can collaborate on notes, databases, calendars, project boards
  • You can share a readOnly public link of any pg you want
  • Collaborative, structured text; workplaces for sharing/editing docs; rich set of embeddable obs (gists, codePens, figma, gsuite, youtube, others); AirTable-like db support (pages are recs); sort/filter em in diff views (gallery/board/etc.)

Pros

  • Some ppl use it as a CMS
  • Good rtf support (@rsch). "Much b8r than AirTable"
  • What Slack did 4 chat & GitHub 4 srcCntrl, Notion does 2 keep info org
  • "Just enuff complexity 2 config it 2 yr prefs but still simple on the surface" same guy wrote 4 power users here
  • Love their docLinks (@rsch) keeps ID regardless of changes + shows title in url.

Cons

    Some ppl left coz
  • Very slow on iPhone: 7 secs to begin typing a note
  • No offline support @ all
  • No offline srch support; attachments stored in S3 unavail offline.
  • Bad exporting support
  • Paying cust.s begging "pls fix the search"
  • Many complaints about their Login sys: every login you get a random code sent to your email; they claim it's more secure than nm/pwd
  • Bad migration path from EverNote. "Many unhappy EverN usrs wd kill for a smooth migration path" You lose folders/imgs
  • User said major complaint was Drawing Support (draw.io integ.) + inline math support. However, same user solved & posted on Notion's site under hacks
  • 'Pro' mind-map usr unsatisfied; says nothing else either, uses pen/paper for his "extensive tag vocab"
  • "Cld really use something like this on desktop if they had Groove-like peer2peer sync" (Others also compare Notion 2 Notes) "Notes not dead, got reinvented as Notion"
  • "Can't use anything today that's not end-2-end encrypted"
  • The whole UI splattered w/emojis
  • "Not forgettable" ie right 2 be forgotten after removing data: gdpr See this for more info/rsch on providing this for whoever needs it.
  • The more features added more time I was spending "perfecting" my wrkspace than doing actual work. Nice UX but 2 complicated

Marketing

  • Began to take traction around 2018 in YouTube's thriving productivity subculture (where Videos of fans swapping time mngt tips & org guides rack up millions of views)
  • After that, following snowballed. More than 275k ppl joined a dedicated subreddit, 10ks share free templates in private FB grps; many TikTok vdos advising usrs how to make their Notion pgs prettier (watched 100m+ times)
  • Poster was using free student cp; when working @ co suggested Notion: "That's exactly how freemium in b2b Saas works"

Pricing

  • Postered asked whether they were "going after Jira" with pricing @ 10/usr; someone replied that w/Jira, for 25 users it becomes $2500 ie $1/usr becomes $100/usr (B2B pricing)
  • Another user (huh?): '@least Notion is a legit enterprise SaaS prod with a trial tier, not a free consumer prod attempting to go enterprise.'
  • 2020: new plan unlimited pgs, upto 5 guests free.
